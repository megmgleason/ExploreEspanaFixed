OUR MISSION

Here at Explore España, we believe in hands on learning. We strive to provide an spanish-only environment for Spanish students to practice their skills in a practical, 'real world' situation. We created a scavenger hunt to be used with Virtual Reality on the streets of Spain for students to complete, and at the end the user must guess what city they're in to recieve a prize.
